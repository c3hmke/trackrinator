# trackrinator

[![CC BY-NC-SA 4.0][cc-by-nc-sa-shield]][cc-by-nc-sa]

---

A simple workout tracker focussed on tracking weights.
This because the majority of options out there either are trying to do too much or are ad-infested malware.

This app simply allows you to adjust a coupl settings to get going then gets out of your way and gets on with it.
The goal here is to track in the same way you would with pen and paper with just the slightest touch of automation,
explicitly NOT to get in the way of or dictate how you do things.
